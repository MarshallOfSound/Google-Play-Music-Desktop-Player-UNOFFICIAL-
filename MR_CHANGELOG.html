This is a patch release of Google Play Music Desktop Player
YouTube Music Support

You can now listen to YouTube Music by choosing the "YouTube Music" option in the tray menu.

Please note that support for YouTube Music is in it's early stages, the core functionality works but things like Theming and the Mini Player do not yet work.
